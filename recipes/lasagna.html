Lasagna

Description

An italian classic! Little is more satisfying than cooking a classic to perfection, Sarah Cook gives us a lesson in how to make lasagne.

Ingredients

Meat sauce

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot (about 100g), finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 500g beef mince (we used 10% fat)
  • 500g pork mince or British veal mince
  • 200ml milk
  • 2 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 rosemary sprig
  • 2 thyme sprigs
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 500ml red wine

Bechamel

  • 1.5l milk
  • 1 onion, thickly sliced
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 3 cloves
  • 100g butter
  • 100g plain flour
  • good grating of nutmeg

Pasta

  • about 400g dried pasta sheets
  • 50g parmesan, finely grated
